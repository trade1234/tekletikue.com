About Us

Tekle Tikue Import and Export Company was established in 2007. When it started operation, our company was involved in processing and exporting different types of agricultural products in addition to coffee. However, since 2014 our company has made coffee it’s prime focus. We Tekle Tikue Import and Export Co. are specialized in preparing and exporting quality Ethiopian highland Arabica coffee to the world market. So far our main destinations are Europe, Australia, USA and Middle East. Currently our company is considered as one of the most reliable coffee exporting companies in Ethiopia.

  • Exporting Ethiopian Coffee around the world
  • Import product Like PET and PVC resine, Reinforcemnt bars, Trucks, Motorcycle, Tyre and Wheat.
